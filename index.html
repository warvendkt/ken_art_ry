Read my story

Ken Harry Cledjo, born in Benin

I am a French artist. I am passionate about creating art. From a very early age, I have always seen empty spaces as opportunities. Whether it was the margins of my schoolwork, pieces of paper, or even walls, every blank space has been an invitation to create. My initial forays into the world of art began with the creation of playful doodles. These evolved into graffiti, a medium that allowed me to experiment with bold lines, dynamic forms and the raw energy of street art.

However, I have never imposed limitations on myself. I have extensive experience with graphite, having honed my skills to ensure precision and intricate detail. I have also gained proficiency in oil and acrylic, working with their rich textures. Today, my artistic creations are displayed on various surfaces, including canvas, wood, and walls, reflecting the inspiration of the moment. The rose, my signature motif, is a recurring theme in my work. It symbolizes beauty, resilience, and transformation.

I am currently based in New York , where I am pushing the boundaries of art. I blend my roots, experiences, and emotions into every stroke. In my professional opinion, the value of art extends beyond its creation; it encompasses the ability to tell a story, establish connections, and leave a lasting impact. The journey continues to unfold, and I am pleased to report that I have just begun it.
