Read my story

Ken Art Ry, born Ken Harry Cledjo in Benin

I am a French artist with an unstoppable passion for creation. Since childhood, I’ve seen every blank space as an invitation—whether it was the margins of my homework, scraps of paper, or even walls. What started as playful doodles quickly evolved into graffiti, where I experimented with bold lines, dynamic forms, and the raw energy of street art.

But I never limited myself. Over time, I dove into the precision of graphite, mastering intricate details, and then expanded into the rich textures of oil and acrylic. Today, my art lives on multiple surfaces—canvas, wood, walls—wherever inspiration takes me. My signature motif, the rose, blooms throughout my work, symbolizing beauty, resilience, and transformation.

Now based in New York, I push the boundaries of art, blending my roots, experiences, and emotions into every stroke. To me, art isn’t just about creating—it’s about storytelling, connecting, and leaving a lasting impact. The journey is limitless, and I’m just getting started.
