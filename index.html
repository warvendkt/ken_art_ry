Read my story

Ken Art Ry, born Ken Harry Cledjo in January 1999 in Benin

is a French artist with a deep, lifelong connection to art. From a young age, he would fill the margins of his homework books with imaginative drawings, using every blank space as a canvas for his creativity. This passion soon evolved into graffiti on paper, where he experimented with bold lines and forms, eventually transitioning to graffiti on walls, bringing his ideas to life in larger, more expressive ways.

Over time, his artistic journey led him to the precision of pencil drawings, capturing intricate details and refining his craft. Finally, he found his true calling in painting, using oil and acrylic to tell stories that resonate deeply with viewers. His signature motif, the rose, blooms across genres and styles, symbolizing beauty, resilience, and transformation. Now based in New York, Ken Art Ry continues to explore the limitless possibilities of art, blending his roots, experiences, and vision into every piece he creates. templatemo. Thank you.
